Puppy Travel Preparation Checklist for Blue Staffy Puppies UK‘s Families

To ensure your blue Stafford terrier puppy settles in quickly and comfortably after its journey, here’s a simple checklist for families awaiting their new arrival:

Designated Safe Space

Have a quiet, warm, and secure area ready with a comfy bed for your puppy to decompress.

Food & Water Bowls

Prepare fresh water and a bowl with the same food your puppy has been eating (we provide a starter pack and a list of the best dog food brands for Staffy puppies UK!)

Essential Supplies

Ensure you have puppy-safe toys, a suitable collar/harness, and a lead.

Vet Appointment

Schedule your first vet check-up within 48-72 hours of arrival for their final health check.

Potty Spot

Identify a designated outdoor area for immediate potty pad breaks upon arrival.

Patience & Calm

Approach their arrival with calm energy; they may be a little tired initially.

Is the puppy accompanied during transport?

Yes, absolutely. Our DEFRA-approved pet couriers specialise in animal transportation, meaning your puppy is always attended to and monitored throughout its journey, ensuring comfort and safety.

What do I need to prepare for my puppy’s arrival via delivery?

Please refer to our “Puppy Travel Preparation Checklist for Families” above for essential tips on making your home ready for a smooth arrival and ensuring your blue Staffy puppies available settle in quickly.

Yes, our couriers provide updates during transit. We will communicate the tracking process and estimated arrival times so you are fully informed as your English Staffordshire bull terrier makes its way to you.
